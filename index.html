FAQ

Answers before guests message.

Can I order without knowing the exact menu?

Yes. Send the date, guest count, area, and budget — Mariane will suggest a balanced menu.

Do you cater big weddings and corporate events?

Yes — we regularly cater events for 50–500+ guests including buffet setup, servers, chafing dishes, and dessert tables.

How much notice do you need?

48–72 hours for small orders, 2–4 weeks for big events. Last-minute is sometimes possible — message us to check.

Where is the kitchen located?

Cleopatra, Alexandria. The exact address is shared after confirmed pickup orders.

Can the menu handle allergies or vegetarian guests?

Yes — share dietary notes before the quote so the menu is planned responsibly.

How does payment work?

A deposit confirms the order (50% for big events). Balance is due on delivery. InstaPay, Vodafone Cash, bank transfer, card, or cash.

Do you deliver outside Alexandria?

Yes — North Coast in summer is regular, Cairo by request with delivery quoted separately.
