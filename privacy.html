Privacy policy

How Sofret Mariane handles customer information.

Last updated: May 25, 2026.

Information we collect

When you request a quote or place an order, we collect the details you provide: name, phone number, event date, guest count, area, menu notes, cart items, and chosen payment method.

How we use it

We use this information to prepare quotes, confirm menus, coordinate delivery, and support customer service through WhatsApp or direct contact.

Payments

The website does not store card details. Card, bank, InstaPay, and wallet details are confirmed manually on WhatsApp until a verified payment gateway is configured.

Local storage

The app may store cart and recent order details locally on your device so the order flow works smoothly. You can clear this from your browser or app storage settings.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact Sofret Mariane on WhatsApp.
