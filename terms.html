Order terms for catering customers.

Last updated: May 25, 2026.

Quotes

Website prices are planning estimates. Final prices depend on guest count, delivery area, ingredient cost, service style, and final menu confirmation.

Deposits

Orders are confirmed only after Mariane confirms the menu and deposit terms on WhatsApp or direct contact.

Cancellations

Deposit refunds are available up to 7 days before the event unless custom ingredients or special production have already been purchased.

Payments

Cash on delivery is supported. Other payment methods are confirmed manually until verified merchant accounts are configured.
